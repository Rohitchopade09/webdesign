  • 1630-45 - Upbringing under the guidance of mother Jijabai and teacher Dadaji Kondev
  • 1640 - Saibai and Shivaji Maharaj were married while still in their childhood.
  • 1645
  • 1659 - Shivaji Maharaj killed Afzal Khan at the foot of Pratapgad fort
  • 1674 -Shivaji Maharaj was crowned king of the Marathas in a lavish ceremony at Raigad.
  • 1680 -Shivaji Maharaj died at the age of 52, on the eve of Hanuman Jayanti
